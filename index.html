THE STORY

Toji Zenin, the legendary assassin with no cursed energy. Once bound to the Zenin clan, she has freed herself and now works as a powerful independent operative.

Multiple targets have appeared across the Jujutsu world - sorcerers who stand in your way. Each mission brings you closer to your ultimate goal.

Navigate through dangerous areas, eliminate targets with precision, and collect cursed tools to enhance your abilities.
